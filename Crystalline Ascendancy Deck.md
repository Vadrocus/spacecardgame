# Crystalline Ascendancy Deck

*The Crystalline Ascendancy represents a silicon-based consciousness that evolved to harness energy through crystalline structures. Their strategy revolves around harmony, resonance, and interconnectedness, allowing them to function as a collective intelligence that grows stronger when properly aligned.*

## Capital Ships
1. **Shard Dreadnought**
   - Type: Capital Ship
   - Cost: 5 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 4, Defense 4, Speed 1
   - Ability: Can be equipped with *Prismatic Beam* for enhanced attack capabilities
   - Special: When connected to Crystal Lattice Network, gains +1 to all stats

2. **Crystalline Carrier**
   - Type: Capital Ship
   - Cost: 5 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 3, Defense 5, Speed 1
   - Ability: Can deploy Crystal Shard Scouts at no Energy cost once per turn

3. **Resonance Flagship**
   - Type: Capital Ship
   - Cost: 6 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 5, Defense 3, Speed 1
   - Ability: Amplifies Harmony gain for all Crystalline cards by +1 per turn

4. **Geode Leviathan**
   - Type: Capital Ship
   - Cost: 6 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 3, Defense 6, Speed 1
   - Ability: Can absorb energy attacks and redirect them on the next turn

## Scout & Support Ships
5. **Shard Interceptor**
   - Type: Scout Ship
   - Cost: 2 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 1, Defense 1, Speed 3
   - Ability: When targeted by *Phase Shift*, becomes intangible for one round

6. **Prismatic Harvester**
   - Type: Mining Ship
   - Cost: 3 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 0, Defense 2, Speed 1
   - Ability: Gathers 1 Energy resource per turn from asteroid or debris fields

7. **Crystal Emissary**
   - Type: Science Ship
   - Cost: 3 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 0, Defense 2, Speed 2
   - Ability: Increases Harmony by 1 when scanning artifacts or ruins

8. **Facet Explorer**
   - Type: Scout Ship
   - Cost: 1 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 0, Defense 1, Speed 3, Scan 1
   - Ability: Can scan through obstacles and interference

## Space Stations
9. **Crystal Nexus**
   - Type: Space Station
   - Cost: 3 Energy
   - Stats: Defense +2
   - Ability: Generates 1 Energy per turn, can store up to 3 excess Energy

10. **Crystal Cathedral**
    - Type: Space Station (Upgraded)
    - Cost: 5 Energy
    - Stats: Defense +4
    - Ability: Generates 3 Energy per turn, amplifies crystal-based effects by 25%

11. **Harmonic Transponder**
    - Type: Space Station
    - Cost: 4 Energy
    - Stats: Defense +3
    - Ability: Allows instant redeployment of one Crystalline unit between controlled zones per turn

12. **Geode Foundry**
    - Type: Space Station
    - Cost: 4 Energy
    - Stats: Defense +3
    - Ability: Creates one Crystal Fragment token per turn (can be spent as 1 Energy for crystal cards only)

## Surface Units
13. **Crystal Guardians**
    - Type: Surface Unit
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Stats: Attack 2, Defense 2
    - Ability: Adjacent Crystalline structures gain +1 Defense

14. **Geode Excavator**
    - Type: Surface Unit
    - Cost: 2 Energy
    - Stats: Attack 1, Defense 1, Mining +1
    - Ability: Can discover resources and artifacts in planetary regions

15. **Shard Artillery**
    - Type: Surface Unit
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Stats: Attack 3, Defense 1, Range 2
    - Ability: Can target orbital units from the surface

16. **Geode Sentinels**
    - Type: Surface Unit
    - Cost: 4 Energy
    - Stats: Attack 2, Defense 4
    - Ability: Counter-attack against any unit that attacks adjacent Crystalline structures

17. **Harmonic Resonator**
    - Type: Surface Unit
    - Cost: 4 Energy
    - Stats: Attack 0, Defense 2
    - Ability: Can disrupt enemy structures by matching resonant frequencies

## Planetary Structures
18. **Resonance Amplifier**
    - Type: Structure (Planetside)
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Stats: Defense 2
    - Ability: Enhances communication between Crystalline units, improves scan attempts

19. **Consciousness Nexus**
    - Type: Structure (Planetside)
    - Cost: 5 Energy
    - Stats: Defense 3
    - Ability: Grants one additional action per turn to any Crystalline card

20. **Crystal Monolith**
    - Type: Structure (Planetside)
    - Cost: 4 Energy
    - Stats: Defense 5
    - Ability: Blocks opponent movement in sector, grows stronger each turn (+1 Defense per turn)

21. **Crystal Garden**
    - Type: Structure (Planetside)
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Stats: Defense 2
    - Ability: Generates 1 Energy per turn, slowly spreads crystal growth to adjacent sectors

## Orbital Structures
22. **Crystal Lattice Network**
    - Type: Structure (Orbital)
    - Cost: 4 Energy
    - Stats: Defense 3
    - Ability: Connects all Crystalline units, allowing shared abilities and increasing Harmony by 1 per turn

23. **Phase Disruptor**
    - Type: Structure (Orbital)
    - Cost: 5 Energy
    - Stats: Defense 2
    - Ability: Can neutralize or weaken opponent shields, reducing effectiveness by 50%

24. **Prismatic Deflector**
    - Type: Structure (Orbital)
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Stats: Defense +3
    - Ability: Redirects 1 damage from attacks back to the attacker

25. **Consciousness Uplink**
    - Type: Structure (Orbital)
    - Cost: 4 Energy
    - Stats: Defense 2
    - Ability: Allows one Crystalline unit to copy the ability of another Crystalline unit per turn

## Equipment & Upgrades
26. **Prismatic Beam**
    - Type: Weapon System
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Ability: Equipped ship gains +2 Attack and piercing capability (ignore 1 point of Defense)
    - Equip: Any Capital Ship

27. **Crystalline Armor**
    - Type: Upgrade
    - Cost: 2 Energy
    - Ability: Target gains +2 Defense and regenerates 1 damage per turn
    - Equip: Any Crystalline unit or structure

28. **Harmonic Frequency Modulator**
    - Type: Equipment
    - Cost: 2 Energy
    - Ability: Increases Harmony generation by +1 per turn
    - Equip: Any Crystalline structure

29. **Phase Crystal**
    - Type: Equipment
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Ability: Equipped unit can phase through obstacles and ignore planetary effects
    - Equip: Any Crystalline unit

## Event Cards
30. **Crystal Resonance**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 0 Energy
    - Effect: Create a protective barrier around target Crystalline card, reducing incoming damage

31. **Consciousness Merge**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: Special (Sacrifice one Crystalline unit)
    - Effect: Transfer all power and abilities to repair or enhance another Crystalline unit

32. **Phase Shift**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 2 Energy
    - Effect: Target Crystalline unit becomes temporarily intangible, cannot be targeted by attacks or scans until end of round

33. **Shield Matrix**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Effect: Create a shield around all Crystalline units in a sector, granting +1 Defense until next turn

34. **Crystal Growth**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 1 Energy
    - Effect: Begin repair process on damaged Crystalline unit or structure, completing in 1 turn if not disrupted

35. **Crystal Storm**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 3 Energy (or 1 if played from Crystalline structure)
    - Effect: Blanket the battlefield with prismatic shards, disrupting all sensors and communications (-2 to scan attempts)

36. **Crystalline Transcendence**
    - Type: Event (Rare)
    - Cost: Special (Channel full power of Crystal Cathedral)
    - Effect: Transform all Crystal units into a higher state of being, advancing toward consciousness victory

37. **Consciousness Ascension**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 4 Energy
    - Effect: Raise Harmony by the number of Crystalline structures in play

38. **Total Harmony**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 2 Energy
    - Effect: Create impenetrable field around target by achieving resonance between multiple Crystalline structures

39. **Consciousness Upload**
    - Type: Event (Rare)
    - Cost: Special (Commit all Crystalline consciousness)
    - Effect: Transfer collective consciousness to a target artifact or planetary system

40. **Prismatic Refraction**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 2 Energy
    - Effect: Clone one Crystalline ability from any card in play and apply it to target Crystalline unit for one turn

## Artifact Cards
41. **Resonance Key**
    - Type: Artifact
    - Acquisition: Discovered in crystal formations with Mining or Science unit
    - Ability: Amplifies all Crystal effects by 50% when defending against technology

42. **Ancient Crystal Matrix**
    - Type: Artifact
    - Acquisition: Found in planetary ruins, requires harmonizing with crystal frequencies
    - Ability: Store up to 5 Energy that can only be used for Crystalline Transcendence

43. **Planetary Consciousness Core**
    - Type: Artifact (Rare)
    - Acquisition: Located in neutral territory, requires control of planetary sector
    - Ability: Special win condition enabler when combined with Crystalline consciousness
