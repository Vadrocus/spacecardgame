# Terran Consensus Card Art Descriptions

## Capital Ships

1. **Hyperion**
   - Style: Retro-futuristic technical illustration like John Berkey
   - Description: A massive battlecruiser with elongated hull and multiple artillery turrets. The ship features distinctive blue engine glows against the backdrop of a gas giant. The design combines sleek curves with industrial elements, showing battle scars and active weapon systems. Dramatic side lighting highlights the detailed plating and structural elements.

2. **Nova-class Battlecruiser**
   - Style: Cinematic sci-fi concept art like Dylan Cole
   - Description: An imposing warship with a hammerhead design and visible weapons arrays. The ship is captured mid-firing its main cannon, with blue-white energy cascading from multiple points. The background shows a distant planet partially eclipsed by its moon. The ship's hull has distinctive geometric patterns with Terran insignia visible on its flanks.



3. **Liberator Carrier**
   - Style: Clean technical render like Syd Mead
   - Description: A wide, flat carrier vessel with multiple launch bays visible along its sides. Small fighter craft can be seen launching from its ventral hangars. The design emphasizes function over form with a utilitarian aesthetic featuring exposed docking mechanisms and communication arrays. The ship's profile has a distinctive asymmetrical silhouette against a starfield.

4. **Dreadnought Executor**
   - Style: Epic space opera concept art like Ralph McQuarrie
   - Description: A colossal dagger-shaped capital ship with a massive forward cannon. The ship dwarfs nearby vessels and is captured powering up its main weapon, with energy coursing along conduits on its hull. The vessel features angular geometry with reinforced plating and atmospheric entry capabilities evident in its design. Dramatic lighting emphasizes its intimidating scale.

## Scout & Support Ships

5. **Artemis**
   - Style: Sleek digital concept art like Sparth (Nicolas Bouvier)
   - Description: A nimble reconnaissance vessel with swept-back wings and advanced sensor arrays. The ship has a distinctive glass cockpit bubble and stealth elements in its design. The hull features adaptive camouflage patterns partially engaging. The background shows a nebula with the ship appearing to navigate through a field of stellar debris.

6. **Apollo**
   - Style: Clean technical blueprint style like Ron Cobb
   - Description: A specialized science vessel with extensive external sensor equipment and minimal weapons. The ship has a modular design with detachable research pods and extensive communication equipment. The hull is predominantly white with blue accents and scientific markings. Multiple satellite dishes and scanner arrays dot its exterior, with crew visible through panoramic observation windows.

7. **Valkyrie Dropship**
   - Style: Gritty military sci-fi like Chris Foss
   - Description: A sturdy, armored transport craft designed for atmospheric entry. The ship has visible thrust vectoring engines, troop deployment ramps, and reinforced hull plating. The design emphasizes functionality with visible landing gear, attachment points, and defensive weaponry. The craft is shown descending through clouds toward a rocky, mountainous terrain.

8. **Hermes Courier**
   - Style: Minimalist sci-fi like Moebius (Jean Giraud)
   - Description: A sleek, compact messenger ship with emphasis on speed. The design features aerodynamic lines, oversized engines, and minimal armaments. The ship has a distinctive single-pilot cockpit with enhanced visibility and data relay equipment. The color scheme uses clean whites with accent colors marking its purpose as a communications vessel.

## Space Stations

9. **Guardian-class Defense Platform**
   - Style: Detailed isometric technical illustration like Stephen Biesty
   - Description: An orbital defense station with multiple weapon emplacements and shield generators. The station has a central command module surrounded by defensive arrays in a star pattern. The design includes visible internal compartments showing crew operations and power distribution systems. The station is positioned in low orbit over a partially visible planet surface.

10. **Orbital Command**
    - Style: Cinematic hyperrealism like Peter Elson
    - Description: A large hexagonal command center with extensive communication arrays and docking facilities. The station features a central glass-domed command bridge with spectacular views of the planet below. The exterior shows multiple landing bays, habitat rings, and defensive systems. The human element is emphasized with visible crew working throughout the structure.

11. **Research Station Alpha**
    - Style: Clean retrofuturism like Robert McCall
    - Description: A scientific outpost with multiple observatory domes and experimental apparatus. The station has a wheel design for artificial gravity with spoke-like connectors to a central zero-G laboratory. The exterior features solar arrays, telescopes, and specialized research equipment. The background shows stars and cosmic phenomena being studied by the station's instruments.

12. **Orbital Shipyard**
    - Style: Industrial concept art like Ralph McQuarrie
    - Description: A massive construction facility with ships in various stages of assembly. The shipyard features enormous robotic arms, manufacturing modules, and raw material processing units. A partially completed capital ship is visible in the main construction bay. The facility bustles with activity as worker drones and maintenance craft swarm around the structure.

## Surface Units

13. **Survey Team Alpha**
    - Style: Painted illustration like Frank Frazetta
    - Description: A group of armored explorers examining alien ruins on a windswept plain. The team carries advanced scanning equipment and defensive weapons as they investigate a partially buried alien structure. Their environment suits feature Terran insignia and specialized scientific tools. The lighting captures the harsh alien sun casting long shadows as the team documents their findings.

14. **Vanguard Elite**
    - Style: Military sci-fi concept art like Wayne Barlowe
    - Description: Heavily armored shock troops with advanced weaponry storming a defensive position. The soldiers wear powered exoskeletons with integrated weapon systems and tactical displays. Their armor features adaptive camouflage partially engaged and distinctive insignia denoting elite status. The composition shows them in dynamic combat poses amid alien terrain.

15. **Combat Engineers**
    - Style: Detailed technical concept art like Craig Mullins
    - Description: Specialized troops with equipment designed for battlefield construction and repair. The engineers operate advanced fabrication tools and portable shield generators. Their armor is more utilitarian than combat troops with multiple tool attachments and diagnostic equipment. The scene shows them repairing damaged defensive structures while under partial cover.

16. **Science Corps**
    - Style: Bright sci-fi illustration like Angus McKie
    - Description: Civilian specialists in lightweight environmental suits collecting samples from alien flora. The scientists use holographic interfaces and automated sample collection drones. Their equipment emphasizes data collection rather than combat capabilities with extensive sensor packages. The background shows a vibrant alien ecosystem with unusual plant formations and atmospheric conditions.

## Planetary Structures

17. **Mobile Research Lab**
    - Style: Cutaway technical illustration like Hans Jenssen
    - Description: A modular wheeled laboratory with deployable research equipment. The structure features a transparent dome section housing scientific equipment and specimen analysis stations. The design shows both exterior details and a cutaway revealing the interior layout and scientists at work. The lab is positioned in an alien landscape with equipment deployed around its perimeter.

18. **Advanced Research Complex**
    - Style: Architectural concept art like Syd Mead
    - Description: A sprawling scientific installation with multiple specialized research domes. The complex combines function and form with elegant structural elements supporting various research apparatus. The facility includes habitat sections, power generation, and defense systems integrated into a cohesive design. Atmospheric effects show how the structure interacts with the alien environment.

19. **Planetary Shield Generator**
    - Style: Cinematic sci-fi scene like Doug Chiang
    - Description: A massive energy projection facility with three enormous emitter towers. The generator shows energy visibly coalescing at the tower peaks before expanding outward. The design incorporates cooling systems, power distribution networks, and control facilities. The partial atmospheric shield is visible as a translucent dome extending from the generators into the sky above.

20. **Terraforming Control Node**
    - Style: Environmental concept art like Stephan Martiniere
    - Description: A control center surrounded by visible environmental transformation effects. The structure features monitor arrays showing planetwide data and atmospheric composition readings. The immediate vicinity shows accelerated plant growth and atmospheric processors at work. Energy fields extend from the central spire, visibly altering the surrounding landscape in concentric circles.

## Orbital Structures

21. **Temporal Distortion Array**
    - Style: Abstract technical art like Peter Elson
    - Description: A bizarre geometric structure with components that appear to exist in multiple states simultaneously. The array features crystalline elements suspended in electromagnetic fields with energy coursing between nodes. The surrounding space shows visual distortion effects with light bending around the structure. The design deliberately challenges conventional physics with impossible angles and temporal echoes.

22. **Defense Grid**
    - Style: Hard sci-fi technical illustration like Ron Miller
    - Description: A network of interconnected orbital platforms generating a protective energy mesh. The grid consists of multiple smaller units working in concert to project energy barriers between them. The design emphasizes efficient energy distribution with power conduits visible between nodes. The protective barrier is shown intercepting incoming projectiles or energy weapons.

23. **Quantum Observatory**
    - Style: Retro-space age illustration like Chesley Bonestell
    - Description: An orbital telescope array with multiple detection systems studying spatial anomalies. The observatory features distinctive dish arrays, quantum entanglement detectors, and computing clusters. The structure captures the wonder of cosmic exploration with dramatic positioning near a stellar phenomenon. The design includes human observation decks providing scale to the massive instruments.

24. **Orbital Factory**
    - Style: Industrial sci-fi like John Harris
    - Description: An automated manufacturing facility processing asteroid materials in orbit. The factory features massive refineries, assembly lines, and material storage areas. Raw resources enter one end while completed components emerge from the other. The design emphasizes industrial processes with conveyor systems, robotic assemblers, and quality control stations visible throughout the structure.

## Equipment & Upgrades

25. **Quantum Sensor**
    - Style: Technical close-up illustration like Katsuhiro Otomo
    - Description: A sophisticated scanning device with multiple lenses and quantum field detectors. The device shows internal components through translucent casing with holographic readouts hovering above its surface. The design incorporates both advanced technology and ergonomic considerations for field use. Energy patterns visibly flow through the device's circuitry as it processes data.

26. **Shield Generator**
    - Style: Detailed cross-section technical art like Hans Jenssen
    - Description: A compact energy projection system with visible power core and emitter array. The generator shows both exterior casing and internal components in a technical cutaway view. The device projects a visible energy field that contours to the shape of what it's protecting. Power distribution systems and cooling mechanisms are clearly illustrated in the technical rendering.

27. **Quantum Warheads**
    - Style: Military technical illustration like Ian McQue
    - Description: Advanced weapon systems with reality-warping destructive capabilities. The warheads feature distinctive warning markings and security systems to prevent unauthorized use. The design shows both the delivery mechanism and the exotic materials contained within. The scale of the weapons is emphasized by including human figures for reference near the storage racks.

28. **Advanced AI Core**
    - Style: Cyberpunk digital art like Josan Gonzalez
    - Description: A neural network processor with holographic interface showing decision trees. The core features cooling systems necessary for quantum computing operations and security protocols. The design shows data visualizations emerging from the processing unit with human operators monitoring its functions. Light patterns suggest sentient activity within the crystalline structure of the processor.

31. **EMP Pulse**
    - Style: Technical effect visualization like Moebius
    - Description: An expanding electromagnetic wave disabling electronic systems in its path. The pulse originates from a compact device with energy rippling outward in concentric blue rings. Enemy systems visibly short-circuit and power down as the wave passes over them. The illustration uses cross-section elements to show how the pulse affects different technologies.

    - Style: Space opera action scene like Chris Foss
    - Description: Multiple ships emerging from a gate formation in a dramatic arrival moment. The reinforcement fleet arrives in perfect formation with energy still dissipating from interstellar travel. The design emphasizes the coordinated nature of the Terran military response with ships of various classes arriving simultaneously. The background shows the tactical situation they're arriving to address.

33. **Final Protocol**
    - Style: Dramatic moment like John Harris
    - Description: A technician activating an emergency system with visible gravity to the moment. The scene shows the final safeguard being removed and the activation sequence initiating. The design emphasizes the significance of the action with warning symbols, multiple confirmation systems, and the determined expression of the operator. Energy begins cascading through previously dormant systems as the protocol activates.

34. **Emergency Evacuation**
    - Style: Tense action scene like Philippe Druillet
    - Description: Personnel being rapidly extracted from a deteriorating situation. The evacuation shows transport vessels loading under duress with priority materials and personnel. The scene captures the organized urgency of Terran evacuation protocols amid environmental hazards. The composition directs focus to both the evacuees and the danger they're escaping.

35. **Orbital Bombardment**
    - Style: Epic scale destruction like Karel Thole
    - Description: Multiple orbital platforms coordinating a wide-area attack on a planetary surface. The bombardment consists of dozens of energy beams converging on a target area from different orbital positions. The attack creates visible atmospheric disturbances as the beams cut through cloud layers. The scale of the operation is emphasized by showing both the orbital platforms and the extensive surface effects.

36. **Quantum Entanglement**
    - Style: Abstract scientific visualization like Donato Giancola
    - Description: Two separate Terran systems becoming synchronized through quantum effects. The entanglement is visualized as energy patterns that mirror each other despite physical separation. The illustration shows both the scientific principle and its tactical application in the field. Subtle color shifts indicate the transfer of properties between the linked systems.

37. **Sacrifice Play**
    - Style: Emotional narrative moment like Julie Bell
    - Description: A Terran officer making a fateful decision to divert all power to a critical system. The composition shows both the officer's resolute expression and the consequences of their decision as systems begin shutting down. The design emphasizes the human element of sacrifice amid advanced technology. Energy visibly transfers from the sacrificed system to the receiving system in a last-ditch effort.

38. **Scientific Breakthrough**

The laboratory shows holographic data coalescing into a new understanding with researchers pointing at key elements. The design captures the intellectual triumph with appropriate scientific imagery and equipment. The moment of breakthrough is emphasized by lighting that focuses on the central discovery.

39. **Tactical Maneuvers**
    - Style: Strategic visualization like John Schoenherr
    - Description: A holographic command table showing unit repositioning during an operation. The display shows both friendly and enemy positions with movement paths and tactical options. The design includes command staff analyzing the situation and implementing changes. The visualization uses color coding and symbols consistent with military tactical displays.

40. **Diplomatic Initiative**
    - Style: Formal diplomatic scene like Frank R. Paul
    - Description: Terran representatives engaging with alien counterparts in a negotiation chamber. The diplomatic meeting takes place in a neutral space with appropriate ceremonial elements from both cultures. The design emphasizes the tension and potential of diplomatic exchange through body language and environmental details. Security personnel remain visible but unobtrusive in the background.

## Artifact Cards

41. **Quantum Encryption Matrix**
    - Style: Abstract alien technology like Richard M. Powers
    - Description: An incomprehensible device of clearly non-human origin with impossible geometry. The artifact appears to phase between multiple states with information patterns visible within its structure. The design deliberately defies human engineering principles while suggesting immense computational capability. Researchers visible in the scene provide scale and show attempts to interface with the technology.

42. **Chronometric Stabilizer**
    - Style: Ancient-meets-futuristic like Jim Burns
    - Description: A device that appears simultaneously ancient and beyond current technology. The stabilizer features clockwork-like mechanisms that operate in unusual dimensions with visible temporal effects surrounding it. The design includes both the artifact and the Terran containment systems attempting to harness its capabilities. Temporal distortion is visualized as rippling effects in the local environment.

43. **Planetary Consciousness Core**
    - Style: Mind-bending alien artifact like Paul Lehr
    - Description: A massive crystalline structure buried within planetary crust that pulses with sentient energy. The core features organic-seeming patterns despite its crystalline nature with energy pathways resembling neural networks. The design shows both the artifact and the surrounding excavation revealing its true scale. Human figures near the artifact appear diminished, emphasizing its monumental importance.
