# Terran Consensus Deck

*The Terran Consensus represents humanity's unified expansion into the stars. Their approach combines advanced technology, scientific research, and military precision. The Terran strategy typically focuses on shield technology, research advancement, and tactical flexibility.*

## Capital Ships
1. **Hyperion**
   - Type: Capital Ship
   - Cost: 4 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 3, Defense 2, Speed 1
   - Ability: Can perform Orbital Strike (Attack -1) against surface targets once per turn

2. **Nova-class Battlecruiser**
   - Type: Capital Ship
   - Cost: 5 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 5, Defense 3, Speed 1
   - Ability: Can be equipped with up to 2 weapon systems
   - Special: When equipped with Quantum Warheads, gains +2 Attack against structures

3. **Liberator Carrier**
   - Type: Capital Ship
   - Cost: 5 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 2, Defense 4, Speed 1
   - Ability: Can deploy 2 Scout Ships per turn without gate requirement

4. **Dreadnought Executor**
   - Type: Capital Ship
   - Cost: 6 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 6, Defense 4, Speed 1
   - Ability: Once per game, can fire main cannon to deal 8 damage to any single target

## Scout & Support Ships
5. **Artemis**
   - Type: Scout Ship
   - Cost: 2 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 1, Defense 1, Speed 2, Scan 1
   - Ability: +1 to all Scan attempts

6. **Apollo**
   - Type: Science Ship
   - Cost: 3 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 0, Defense 2, Speed 2, Science 2
   - Ability: Generates 1 Science point per turn when in orbit

7. **Valkyrie Dropship**
   - Type: Support Ship
   - Cost: 2 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 0, Defense 2, Speed 2
   - Ability: Can transport up to 2 Surface Units from orbit to planetside or vice versa

8. **Hermes Courier**
   - Type: Scout Ship
   - Cost: 1 Energy
   - Stats: Attack 0, Defense 1, Speed 3
   - Ability: Draw 1 additional card when this ship is in orbit

## Space Stations
9. **Guardian-class Defense Platform**
   - Type: Space Station
   - Cost: 3 Energy
   - Stats: Defense +3
   - Ability: Protects adjacent ships from first attack each turn

10. **Orbital Command**
    - Type: Space Station/Structure
    - Cost: 4 Energy
    - Stats: Defense +2
    - Ability: Grants +1 to all Terran attempt rolls

11. **Research Station Alpha**
    - Type: Space Station
    - Cost: 4 Energy
    - Stats: Defense +2, Science +2
    - Ability: Generates 2 Science points per turn

12. **Orbital Shipyard**
    - Type: Space Station
    - Cost: 5 Energy
    - Stats: Defense +3
    - Ability: Reduce cost of ship deployment by 1 Energy (minimum 1)

## Surface Units
13. **Survey Team Alpha**
    - Type: Surface Unit
    - Cost: 2 Energy
    - Stats: Attack 1, Defense 1, Scan 2
    - Ability: Can locate hidden resources and artifacts

14. **Vanguard Elite**
    - Type: Surface Unit
    - Cost: 4 Energy
    - Stats: Attack 4, Defense 3
    - Ability: Can attack twice per turn but cannot counter-attack

15. **Combat Engineers**
    - Type: Surface Unit
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Stats: Attack 2, Defense 2
    - Ability: Can repair one damaged structure per turn

16. **Science Corps**
    - Type: Surface Unit
    - Cost: 2 Energy
    - Stats: Attack 0, Defense 1, Science 2
    - Ability: Doubles Science generation of adjacent Research structures

## Planetary Structures
17. **Mobile Research Lab**
    - Type: Structure (Planetside)
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Stats: Defense 2, Science +1
    - Ability: Generates 1 Science point per turn, can be upgraded with Science equipment

18. **Advanced Research Complex**
    - Type: Structure (Planetside)
    - Cost: 5 Energy
    - Stats: Defense 3, Science +3
    - Ability: Generates 3 Science points per turn, advances science-based win conditions

19. **Planetary Shield Generator**
    - Type: Structure (Planetside)
    - Cost: 5 Energy
    - Stats: Defense 4
    - Ability: Can activate up to 3 shield segments, each requiring 1 turn and 2 Energy
    - Win Condition: When all 3 segments are active with no enemy units on the surface, achieve System Control victory

20. **Terraforming Control Node**
    - Type: Structure (Planetside)
    - Cost: 4 Energy
    - Stats: Defense 3
    - Ability: Modifies planetary environment each turn, providing cumulative bonuses to Terran units

## Orbital Structures
21. **Temporal Distortion Array**
    - Type: Structure (Orbital)
    - Cost: 4 Energy
    - Stats: Defense 2
    - Ability: Can delay opponent actions by spending Energy equal to the action's cost

22. **Defense Grid**
    - Type: Structure (Orbital)
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Stats: Defense +2
    - Ability: All Terran structures gain +1 Defense

23. **Quantum Observatory**
    - Type: Structure (Orbital)
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Stats: Defense 2, Scan +2
    - Ability: Reveals one card from opponent's hand each turn

24. **Orbital Factory**
    - Type: Structure (Orbital)
    - Cost: 4 Energy
    - Stats: Defense 3
    - Ability: Produces 2 Energy per turn that must be spent on equipment or upgrades

## Equipment & Upgrades
25. **Quantum Sensor**
    - Type: Equipment
    - Cost: 2 Energy
    - Ability: Adds +2 to Scan attempts and allows scanning through interference
    - Equip: Any ship or structure with Scan capability

26. **Shield Generator**
    - Type: Equipment
    - Cost: 2 Energy
    - Ability: Target gains +2 Defense and can extend shields to one adjacent unit
    - Equip: Any space station or structure

27. **Quantum Warheads**
    - Type: Weapon System
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Ability: Equipped ship gains +2 Attack and ignores 1 point of Defense
    - Equip: Any Capital Ship

28. **Advanced AI Core**
    - Type: Upgrade
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Ability: Equipped unit or structure can perform one additional action per turn
    - Equip: Any Terran card

## Event Cards
29. **Orbital Strike**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 0 Energy (requires Capital Ship)
    - Effect: Capital ship can attack surface unit directly with -1 penalty

30. **Dropship Deployment**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 0 Energy
    - Effect: Move crew from one ship to reinforce a Surface Unit (+1 to all actions this turn)

31. **EMP Pulse**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 2 Energy
    - Effect: Disable target enemy structure or ship for one turn

32. **Reinforcements**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 3 Energy
    - Effect: Draw 2 cards and immediately deploy one unit at -1 cost

33. **Final Protocol**
    - Type: Event (Rare)
    - Cost: Special (Sacrifice one structure)
    - Effect: Activate ultimate ability of target structure without meeting normal requirements

34. **Emergency Evacuation**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 1 Energy
    - Effect: Return target surface unit to orbit, preventing its destruction

35. **Orbital Bombardment**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 3 Energy (or 0 if played from orbital structure)
    - Effect: Deal damage to all enemy surface units and structures in target sector

36. **Quantum Entanglement**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 2 Energy
    - Effect: Link two Terran cards, allowing them to share abilities and bonuses

37. **Sacrifice Play**
    - Type: Event (Rare)
    - Cost: Special (Sacrifice one unit or ship)
    - Effect: Transfer all power and abilities to target structure or unit

38. **Scientific Breakthrough**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 2 Energy
    - Effect: Double Science generation for one turn

39. **Tactical Maneuvers**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 1 Energy
    - Effect: Reposition up to 3 Terran units or ships

40. **Diplomatic Initiative**
    - Type: Event
    - Cost: 2 Energy
    - Effect: Prevent all attacks for one round, draw 1 card

## Artifact Cards
41. **Quantum Encryption Matrix**
    - Type: Artifact
    - Acquisition: Requires 3 Science points to decrypt
    - Ability: Once per turn, prevent an opponent's attempt

42. **Chronometric Stabilizer**
    - Type: Artifact
    - Acquisition: Found in ancient ruins, requires Science ship scan
    - Ability: Enhance temporal effects, allowing reroll of one die per turn

43. **Planetary Consciousness Core**
    - Type: Artifact (Rare)
    - Acquisition: Located in neutral territory, requires control of planetary sector
    - Ability: Special win condition enabler when combined with other technologies
